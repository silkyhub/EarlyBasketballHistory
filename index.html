Early Basketball History

In This Web Page YOU Will Learn About How The Game Developed In It's Early Ages.

Basketball was created in 1891 by James Naismith a physical ed teacher. James was asked to create a sport that you could play indoors during the winter.

Naismith's sport was very different from the basketball we play every day, people still really liked it, they actually liked it so much that colleges around the US started to form leagues just in the first couple of years.

Then the game got introduced to the rest of the world from efforts of the Army and the YMCA.

The game was still a growing sport it didn't matter how popular it was. In the early 1900s it was included for an Olympic demonstration. Three decades later the game was then an Olympic sport, which the US team won.

Basketball has been a major sport since the beginning. I feel like the reason it is, is because it has always been a worldwide sport that anybody could be good at if they put the practice time in. One example of that is when basketball became an Olympic sport, and another one is when college leagues started forming.

Some Fun Facts

  • The NBA was created in 1946
  • The 3 point line actually started in 1979
  • Wilt Chamberlain scored 100 points in the NBA in 1962 which is the current world record for most points scored
  • The team with the most NBA championships are the Boston Celtics with 17 championships in 1957, 1959, 1960, 1961, 1962, 1963, 1964, 1965, 1966, 1968, 1969, 1974, 1976, 1981, 1984, 1986, 2008
  • The tallest NBA players ever were Manute Bol and Gheorghe Muresan both at 7-7
  • The shortest NBA player ever was Muggsy Bogues at 5-3
